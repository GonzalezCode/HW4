Cybersecurity is a field in demand in the age of technology. With more businesses and consumers becoming reliant on technology, the need for secure data grows. Hackers have incentive to steal information for reasons not limited to financial gain, hacktivism, and leak data. With this in mind, companies need to be aware of cyber attacks and how to defend themselves accordingly.

Common attacks include:

  • Phishing
  • Ransomware
  • Malware
  • Social Engineering
