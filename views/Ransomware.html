Ransomware is a form of malware that aims to lockdown a company system until a ransom is paid. This form of ransomware has been devastating for organizations and has seen ransoms paid of over $500,000. Ransomware does not only affect companies however, and therefore average citizens should be aware of potentially harmful links and emails.
